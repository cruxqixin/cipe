Asia-Europe Security Products Expo 2014
2014-01-24
Asia-Europe Security Products Expo 2014
Theme: Green City • Internet of things and Smart City • Police Equipment
 
Introduction
In recent years, combined with the national “Twelfth Five-Year Plan”, many cities’ demand for security equipment and technology become a driving force in the development of China's security industry. Around the "Green City”, "Internet of Things and Smart City " projects, in Beijing, Shanghai , Shenzhen, Chongqing , Wuhan, Jiangxi, Shenyang and other major cities the relevant projects have been gradually improved, security products demand and technology for second and third -tier cities will be an upward trend year by year . The security industry ushered in a new opportunity for development.
In order to enhance intelligent field equipments, to exchange development experiences, China Aerospace Science and Industry Corporation and the China high-tech Industrialization Association jointly organize the " Asia-Europe Security Products Expo 2014" and invite domestic and foreign organizations engaged in the development of intelligent equipment technology and product development, production and distribution of the outstanding enterprises. The exhibition will attract all exhibitors and industry stakeholders to provide a vital marketing, branding, technology exchanging, trade negotiation platform for industry development to provide a panoramic view of the service platform.
