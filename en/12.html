2013 the Fourth China (Tianjin Binhai) International Eco-City Forum Complete Success of Sub-forum “Green Technology and Eco-cities of Ecological and Environmental Protection Project Matching Conferen
2013-11-19

        On September 14th-16th , 2013 in Tianjin Binhai International Convention & Exhibition Center, Green Technology and Eco-cities of Ecological and Environmental Protection Project Matching Conference——the sub-forum of 2013 the Fourth China (Tianjin Binhai) International Eco-City Forum was organized by the National Development and Reform Commission, the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Construction, Tianjin People's Government, China International Economic and Exchange Center and co-sponsored by China International High-tech Exchange Center and China High-tech Industrialization Association Department of Cooperation and Coordination for Industry, Academe and Research.
        Green Innovative Technology is a great support of new eco-city, and on the basis of international eco-city development, clean city technology, Ecological and environmental protection building, green information technology and the use of clean energy, the forum invited experts and entrepreneurs from the United States, France, Canada, Switzerland, China and other countries to share their experience of green technology, new technology and new ideas in the new eco-city development.
        In the forum, Michael Hoffman, Professor of American Academy of Engineering and California Institute of Technology, Gustav R. Grob, President of International Clean Energy Association, Michel Raoust, Co-founder of UMO, Shulin Chen, professor of Washington State University, Brian Xie, president of Association of Europe-China Environmental Entrepreneur and managing Director of Competences & Environmental SAS, Yushi Shen, Microsoft Technical Director, Jason Lo, head of strategy for Honeywell China, Damin Xun, managing director and chairman of HaiYing Security Technology Engineering Co,Ltd, Siyu Ye, senior research scientist of Ballard Power Systems, Dr. Miller, the president of three companies: Fuelcell Propulsion Institute, Vehicle Projects Inc, and Supersonic Tubevehicle LLC, Ms. Lei Zhang, Research Council Officer at National Research Council of Canada, had made wonderful speeches.
        We received active attention and response from the scientists and technologists of domestic green environmental protection from the preparation to the opening of the forum. More than 50 units across the country and more than 200 delegates from the field of ecological and environmental protection shared innovative ideas and best practices, and actively interacted with each other.
This forum gathered elites from the field of green technology at home and abroad, and offered an opportunity to conduct academic research, show technical innovation and explore cooperation and development, as well as won praise from many representatives and enterprises, and further promoted international influence of China (Tianjin Binhai) Eco-City Forum.
        In order to commercialize advanced technology of international experts into China, we also organized “Ecological and Environmental Protection Project Matching Conference ”, issuing a total of more than 20 related projects, promoting the international forefront technologies and projects, introducing projects and technology which had overseas market prospects, promoting technical transformation and upgrades of domestic companies, applying technical cooperation of domestic demanded units and propelling domestic green ecological and environmental protection forward.
        We sought partners in China by overseas colleges invitation, projects release and technology extension. We invited Washington State University, JXR International, Association of Europe-China Environmental Entrepreneur, and UMO to carry more than 20 technologies and projects to participate in this event. More than 30 units like Tianjin Eco-City Energy Investment Construction Co., Ltd., Tianjin Polytechnic University, and nearly 100 representatives came to listen presentations and interact with the promotion experts. After the meeting, 7 Chinese units have cooperation intentions with overseas units in urban waste resources and eco-systems technology, new energy technologies, biofuels and urban intelligent transportation technology and environmental management.
