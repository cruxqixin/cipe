2014 Intelligent Equipment Technology and Applications Exhibition
2014-01-24
2014 Intelligent Equipment Technology and Applications Exhibition
Theme: Intelligent    High-end   Environmental friendly
【Introduction】
Upgrading the manufacturing industry, accelerating the development of strategic emerging industries is the national "Twelfth Five-Year Plan” important tasks. In order to enhance intelligent field equipments, to exchange development experiences, China Aerospace Science and Industry Corporation and the China high-tech Industrialization Association jointly organize the " 2014 Intelligent Equipment Technology and Applications Exhibition" and invite domestic and foreign organizations engaged in the development of intelligent equipment technology and product development, production and distribution of the outstanding enterprises. The exhibition will attract all exhibitors and industry stakeholders to provide a vital marketing, branding, technology exchanging, trade negotiation platform for industry development to provide a panoramic view of the service platform.

Exhibition Scope
Intelligent manufacturing equipment: high-end CNC machine tools and basic manufacturing equipment, intelligent engineering machinery, agricultural machinery efficient, intelligent spinning weaving machinery, environmental protection machinery, coal machinery, critical infrastructure components, components and general components; aerospace equipment manufacturing, machine manufacturing and materials manufacturers, composites manufacturing equipment, automatic auxiliary band / auxiliary wire equipment, machine tools and equipment components .
Intelligent Monitoring Instrumentation: new sensors and systems - new effects, new materials, miniaturization, intelligent, low-power, wireless sensor; intelligent control system - Fieldbus distributed control system (FCS), a large-scale joint network control system high-end programmable control system (PLC), an embedded control system for the equipment, the functional safety monitoring system; intelligent temperature, pressure, flow, level, heat, industrial online analytical instrumentation, intelligent frequency electric actuators, smart valve positioner and high reliability actuators; online MS / laser gas / UV spectra / UV fluorescence / NIR spectroscopy systems, sheet metal processing intelligence Shapemeter, high-speed automated ultrasonic nondestructive detector, creep fatigue properties under special environmental testing equipment, etc. products.
Robotics equipment: security, dangerous operations, rescue and other special robots; welding robots, painting robots, palletizing robots, handling robots, assembly robots, grinding and polishing robots, Cartesian robots, other industrial robots.
New Energy Manufacturing Technology and Equipment: intelligent substation equipment and complete sets, intelligent transmission and distribution equipment and technology, clean energy, nuclear power equipment, photovoltaic power generation equipment, biomass power generation equipment.
