Journey to Photonics West 2013
2013-02-19

From 2nd Feb to 7th Feb 2013, China High-tech Industrialization Association attended Photonics West 2013, negotiating further cooperation with SPIE and investigating the development of foreign companies. 

After the survey, our association learnt the condition of what’s happening in foreign countries and what we can do with foreign companies and associations. Through the Photonics West 2013, our association expended our partners and hoped we can help more and more foreign companies to get realize China and access to China market.

        This time, we got acquainted with such associations as IEEE, SECPHO, OCAHT and discussed the cooperation in our events in China and other countries. And our association also discussed the further cooperation in journal with SPIE. Meanwhile we visited foreign countries in the exhibition, which are willing to expend Chinese market, to invite them to come to China to look for their potential partners and clients. Chinese market is so huge and growing fast. On behalf of our members, our association welcomes foreign companies and association come to China.
