Third International Fiber Optic Sensing Technology and Application Exhibition
2014-01-24
Third International Fiber Optic Sensing Technology and Application Exhibition
 
In recent years, the development of fiber optic sensing technology is one of high-tech application; the application of the technology has been evolved in defense sector and various industrial fields of electricity, petroleum, petrochemical, transportation and construction. With the rapid development of Chinese economy, the optical fiber sensing technology market has gradually formed, and has made large-scale results. Fiber Optic Sensing has faced with a golden opportunity to develop.
Following the success of two "Fiber Optic Sensing Technology and Industry Development Forum", the organizers will invite top international scientists, and expand the joint unit, bring together a number of optical fiber sensing technology applications leading international high-end units, and expect more than 400 experts, business leaders and representatives to attend. The general assembly of industry's technology and special events combining industrialization has great influence.
【Highlights】
• Invite top international scientists to make special reports and discuss hot topics of international frontier.
• Cover domestic and international fiber optic sensing defense units, research institutes, enterprises, and build upstream and downstream industry chain.
• Focus on inviting applications leading units to report and publish the project requirements.
• Provide platform and opportunity for industry experts, academics and business representatives.
