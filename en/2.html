Review of CIPE
2012-10-29

2012China (Beijing) International Photo-electronic Exhibition (CIPE2012) was successfully held during June 4~6, at China International Convention Center

 

"We are very satisfied with CIPE2012 and very grateful for the "B2B" meetings arranged by organizers, which are very helpful to know Chinese enterprises and Chinese market better. We hope we can have more opportunities to participate in your activities and we will bring more Euro enterprises to come to your activities."

-----Mr. PACOMARES François of France JFP Microtechnic

 

"B2B MEETING"

 

"B2B" meeting was the most unique feature of CIPE2012. Cooperated with BCI, CIPE would make more opportunities for international exchange. In CIPE2012, we arranged “B2B” meeting for most exhibitors, which got a good performance.

 

 

Professional Exhibition

 

CIPE2012 attracted more than 300 exhibitors from 13 countries, such as FILR, HGH, THORLABS, Corning, ACCESSLASER, TAMRON, Wuhan Guide and other enterprises in and abroad. With more than 7,000 professional visitors, CIPE2012 has made another milestone. CIPE2012 has 4 main sections: Aerospace Section, Laser and Infrared Section, Optics Section and Optical Communication Section.

 

 

Featured Activities

 

(1) National Symposium on Electro-optic Technology (2012)．

(2) IR-tech Industry and Applications Development Forum．

(3) International Symposium on Advanced Laser Processing．

(4) Promotion Meeting of New Laser Technology and Products．

(5) International Forum on Optical Manufacturing, Testing and Material Application．

(6) International Forum on the Industry Development of Access Network of Next Generation.

 

 

About CIPE2013

 

In June 2013, China (Beijing) International Photo-electronic Exhibition will be held.

 

As the development of photo-electronic industry, in order to provide better services, CIPE2013 will cover all the photo-electronic fields, including laser manufacture and process, lasers, infrared technology, optical technology, optical communications, machine vision and measurement.

 

CIPE 2013 must be your best choice to know Chinese market, explore international market and take part in international exchange!

 

 

We are looking forward to your participate!
