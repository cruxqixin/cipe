International Symposium on Atmospheric Environment Monitoring Technology and Application
2014-01-24
International Symposium on Atmospheric Environment Monitoring Technology and Application
13rd-15th May, 2014 China National Convention Center, Beijing
Temperature rise as a symbol of global climate change is the hot topic of attention around the world.  Atmospheric haze environmental disaster is the problem needed to be solved. In response to this major social development needs, we need to rely on remote sensing technology, full use of existing environmental monitoring networks and conventional monitoring methods, the use of remote sensing technology with ground monitoring as a combination of methods, the integration of multi-temporal, multi data sources (including non-remote sensing data) information and data. Mainly for greenhouse gases, aerosols, trace gases, to achieve quantitative, integrated and systematic testing. For the meteorological department, the environment department and other units need to provide timely and accurate information for government departments to provide scientific and technological support management decisions
In order to promote the development and improvement of this field, organizing committee will invite to more than 20 well-known experts and academic leaders to the meeting as invited papers. Welcome domestic experts in the field, academics, engineers, technicians and graduate students to submit.
