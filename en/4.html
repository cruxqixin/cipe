Exhibition
China International Photoelectrionic Exhibition
2012-10-29

Location:China national convention center

 

Date:Jun 25-27, 2013

 

To continuously encourage international communication and cooperation within the photo-electronic industry, The China High-tech Industrialization Association, Department of Cooperation and Coordination for Industry, Academe and Research has decided to host 2013 China (Beijing) International Photo-electronic Exhibition in Jun 25-27, 2013 at China national convention center together with the SPIE and BCI. The number of estimated exhibitors will be about 500.

 

 

In the past years, China High-tech Industrialization Association together with the SPIE held the China (Beijing) International Photo-electronic Exhibition (CIPE2010 & CIPE2011 & CIPE2012) which attracted more than a hundred exhibitors from China and abroad, including USA, Canada, UK, Germany, France, Switzerland, Netherlands, Israel, etc．The committee has organized a group of exhibitors consisting of subordinates of China High-tech Industrialization Association as the core and other aerospace photo-electronic enterprises. Based on the demands of Chinese aerospace industry，CIPE met the markets needs, offered great opportunities, such as B2B meeting, to the exhibitors for their development．Its benefits on advertisement promotion made a highlight of the exhibition．

 

 

 

At CIPE2013, a series of featured activities will be held as follows.
(1) International Symposium on Photo-electronic Detection an
(2) International Laser Technology Forum
(3) Laser Processing Equipment Buyer Meetings
(4) The Second Infrared Sharon
(5) Optical Manufacturing and Materials B to B Meetings
(6) Machine Vision Industrial Applications Forum and Product
(7) Fiber Optic Sensing Technology and Industry Developmen
(8) Internet of Things and the Smart City Development Forum

 

CIPE2013 is another breakthrough after CIPE2012．Exhibitors will face the buyers and opponents all over the world at CIPE2013 and could gain the market information exactly and immediately.

 

 

As a professional exhibition with unique features in China, CIPE is the best opportunity to demonstrate optics industry, to cultivate Chinese market．We are looking forward to meeting you in CIPE 2013, and share the biggest opportunities brought by the rapid economic growth in China.

 

 

If you want to know Chinese market better, CIPE2013 must be your opportunity to explore Chinese market, make international cooperation and take part in international technology exchange and talented person hunting.

 

 

Exhibits:
1. Lasers, laser materials, processing equipment and systems
2. Laser application (in the industrial, scientific, medical and other fields)
3. IR materials, products and applications
4. Optical instruments, equipment
5. Optical Manufacturing
6. Precision Optical Test / Measurement / Detection
7. Optical Communication / Optical / light sensor
8. Optical / optoelectronic technologies and products
9. Photoelectric load
10. Optoelectronic components (electronic components and materials)
