International Conference on Next Generation Internet and Information Consumption Industry Technology Innovation
2014-01-24
International Conference on Next Generation Internet and Information Consumption Industry Technology Innovation
 
 
Sponsor：
Chinese Society of Astronautics
China High-Tech Industrialization Association
SPIE
Organizers：
Chinese Society of Astronautics Department of Cooperation and Coordination for Industry, Academe and Research, China High-tech Industrialization Association
Chinese Society of Astronautics Photoelectronic Technology Professional Committee
Conference General Chair：
Hequan WU, Academician, Chinese Academy of Engineering
 
 
High lights：
Ø  The first next generation internet and information consumption exchange fair in China which close interacts between industry and technology.
Ø  More than 20 hot issues of information consumption such as Next Generation Internet(NGI), Internet of Things(IOT), Communication Network, Industrial Internet and modern services etc.
Ø  Dozens of top experts and more than a hundred famous scholars and entrepreneurs will report on live, explain core questions of NGI and information consumption.
Ø  Many internet exemplary cities, dozens of typical application cases will exhibit on live.
Ø  Multiple high-end closed forums, supply a communication and cooperation matching platform for high-end people via technology, application and regional economy.
