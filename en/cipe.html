About Photonics China

Photonics China Expo is the only photonics industry and cross-application technology services Exhibition Fair. Organizers are Chinese Society for Optical Engineering and China High-tech Industrialization Association. It is a new brand of China (Beijing) International Photoelectronic exhibition from 2009, International Symposium on Photoelectonic Detection and Imaging (ISPDI) from 1991, Applied Optics and Photonics China(AOPC) from 2015, and China (Beijing) International High-Tech Exhibition Fair from 2014 which were organized by CSOE and CHIA before. It will hold in May each year in China National Convention Center, Beijing, China. And the first year of Photonics China is in 2015.

2017 Photonics China will update to exhibition area of 50000 m2, more than 2,000 exhibitors,100,000 visitors

 

Scale: Exhibition 22,000 m2 ,Expected Exhibitors: more than 600, Expected Visitors 25,000
