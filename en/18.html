2014 China (Beijing) International Solar Energy Exhibition
2014-01-24
2014 China (Beijing) International Solar Energy Exhibition
 
Feature 1: Focus on domestic and international cooperation
The exhibition by multi-form, multi-level, multi-resource procurement matchmaking, new technology, product launches, academic exchanges and other interactive activities is for exhibitors to provide a large number of first-hand information, focusing on the application and improve participation effectively.
Features 2: Traction Convergence Industry Perspective into Town
The national "five" background expanding domestic demand, boost innovation, promote urbanization, regional development, energy conservation, improve public services and economic reform, the transformation of foreign economic development, affecting the solar energy industry chain, deep market potential, jointly organized matchmaking.
Features 3: Convergence Chain Buyers in Many Areas
Keep up with market trends, firstly to build solar products trade sourcing platform. And many domestic and foreign purchasing groups organize site procurement, so that exhibitors directly face with user units.
 
International Forum on The Future Development of Solar Cell Industry
Invite delegates, experts and well-known entrepreneurs, making the forum lively and interactive. Provide a solar cell technology and industry platform to further promote domestic and foreign technology and industry cooperation.
