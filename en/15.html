International Conference on Solar Cell Technology and Application
2014-01-24
International Conference on Solar Cell Technology and Application
2014.5.13-15  Beijing National Convention Center
 
Sponsor：
Chinese Society of Astronautics
China High-Tech Industrialization Association
SPIE
Organizers：

Chinese Society of Astronautics Department of Cooperation and Coordination for Industry, Academe and Research, China High-tech Industrialization Association
Photoelectronic Technology Professional Committee
International exchange center of high and new technology
Supporting Organizations：
Shanghai Institute of Technical Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences
Conference General Chair：
Junhao Chu, Academician, Chinese Academy of Sciences
Medias:
Journal of Infrared and Millimeter Waves (SCI)
Infrared and Laser Engineering (EI)
Expected Scale: 100-150 attendees
 
Topics：
l  Thin film solar cell technology  
l  Organic photovoltaic cells and components 
l  The next generation of nanoscale photonic and battery technology, solar energy conversion
l  High-efficiency Crystalline silicon solar cells technology
l  Silicon heterojunction high-efficiency solar cells technology
l  Flexible and super lightweight solar cells technology
l  The reliability of the pv module components and systems
l  Power technology and the imaging optical system of concentrated solar
l  The use of solar cell technology in space
l  Photovoltaic power generation systems and applications
l  Solar power industrial heat utilization and application in new forms
l  Industrialization process of concentrated solar power technology
l  Photovoltaic solar cells Complementary material accessories technology and application
l  Pv testing technology and industry standard
