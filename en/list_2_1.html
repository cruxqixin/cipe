Chinese Society for Optical Engineering

Chinese Society for Optical Engineering(CSOE)

Introduction

Chinese Society for Optical Engineering is a national society approved by Chinese Ministry of Civil Affairs. Chinese Society for Optical Engineering brings together optic and optical engineering scientists, engineers, educators and business leaders. The membership totals more than 3000 individuals, approximately 600 members reside outside China.

 

Our Idea

·         Adhering to the spirit of "innovation, pragmatism, cooperation, progress”, CSOE establishes a cooperation platform of industry, academe and research, through such ways as academic conference, exhibition and technology transfers to promote technological innovation in optical engineering, focusing on the commercialization of optical technology-oriented applications.

·         CSOE sets up different technology topics in accordance with the directions of the International academic and technology development, while CSOE does not establish secondary branch societies

·         CSOE recommends young leading experts as the main power in this society and trains a number of domestic and foreign new elites and leaders.

 

Department of Cooperation and Coordination for Industry, Academe and Research, China High-tech Industrialization Association

China High-tech Industrialization Association, Department of Cooperation and Coordination for Industry, Academe and Research, is established by Ministry of Science and Technology, Ministry of Commerce and China Aerospace Science & Industry Corporation, is a trans-department, trans-industrial, interdisciplinary and trans-regional national social organization consisting of famous experts, professors, celebrities, social activists, economist, entrepreneurs as well as technology and management workers from scientific community, business world, universities, research institutions and government departments.

We are specialized in aerospace, equipment manufacturing, information technology, security and protection, energy and environment and many other fields. Taking photoelectric technology as the lead and combining the potential market with innovative technology, we aim at promoting high-tech achievement transformation and speeding up high-tech industrialization.

Our objective is to build a bridge among enterprises, universities, research institutions and aerospace units to promote the development of high-tech enterprises by organizing varieties of activities including forums, strategic reports on industrialization, exhibitions and expositions, meetings and negotiations, interactive communication, visiting, studying and training, etc.

Strategic Objective

★Actively promote international intercourse and corporation and establish long-term strategic cooperative partnership with foreign organizations and social groups;

★Hold various large-scale exhibitions, meetings, industry forums, technical training, academic communication and visiting to promote high-tech achievement transformation;

★Promote various exchange activities at home and abroad based on resource and policy advantages of Ministry of Science and Technology, Ministry of Commerce and China Aerospace Science & Industry Corporation.
