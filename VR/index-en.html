    • Introduction

    "Light" with our lives, optical networking and optical information technology is supporting our society. Optical networks have become an important information infrastructure, laying the foundation for the development of smart city, but also to support the next generation of the Internet, the development of mobile Internet, networking, cloud computing and big data and other strategic emerging industries. Application of optical information technology has increasingly penetrated into every aspect of our lives, in many areas of security intelligence, wisdom, health, wisdom transportation, property wisdom, wisdom, home, information consumption, vehicle networking, medical networking, optical information technology has important applications . Optical networks and optical information application technology innovation and industrial development is essential for the development and transformation of China's economy, we must increase investment in research and development , master core technologies, strengthen exchanges and cooperation between the optical network and application of optical information between enterprises, in order to improve product quality to meet the needs of major national strategic development of new industries.
    In this context, the China high-tech Industrialization Association and the China Society for Optical Engineering, jointly held "The Technology Applications Forum of Optical Networks and Optical Information Innovation and Industrial Development", invited industry experts and engineers deliver a keynote speech, covering light Communication with the next generation Internet, fiber-optic sensor networks, optical information and security of life, light and healthy living information, optical information and intellectual life and other industry hot topics of concern to jointly build a research with a large platform.

     Broadband communications, IT, communications and networking, information security, test equipment
     Intelligent communication network systems, intelligent transportation, smart home life, wisdom, energy, intelligent medical
